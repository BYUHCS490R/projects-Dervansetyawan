My Answer

Use an IMG tag when the image is an important part of your content, like a product photo or a picture in an article, because it helps with accessibility and search engines. Use a background image in CSS when the image is only for decoration or style, like a banner or pattern behind text. Image gives meaning or information, background image just makes the page look nice.
