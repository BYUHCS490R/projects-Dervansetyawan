My Favorite Band

Lany is an American pop rock band from Los Angeles that was formed in 2014. The name Lany comes from combining the abbreviations LA (Los Angeles) and NY (New York). I like Lany because the rhythm in some of their songs makes me want to dance, and the lyrics are relatable, emotional, and easy to listen to, with a modern pop sound that feels fresh and personal. A few songs that i like are "DNA", "Dancing in the Kitchen", and "Mean It".

Members of the band :

Paul Jason Klein
He is singer, and lead vocalist of the band.
Jake Goss
He is drummer of the band, playing drums and percussion
Eric Scarborough
He is keyboardist and guitarist of the band.
Kim Vi
He is bassist of the band.
