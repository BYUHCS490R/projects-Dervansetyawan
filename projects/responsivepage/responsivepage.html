DiCaprio's Family

  • Leonardo’s parents, Irmelin and George, are both artistic and helped him love art and movies from a young age.

Family Member

George DiCaprio

George DiCaprio is Leonardo’s father and a creative man who works as an artist and writer. He helped Leonardo learn about art and stories when he was a child. George and Leonardo have a close relationship and often talk about ideas and creativity together.

Irmelin Indenbirken

Irmelin Indenbirken is Leonardo DiCaprio’s loving mother who raised him mostly by herself. She worked as a legal secretary to support her family and give Leonardo a good life. Irmelin is very proud of her son and often goes with him to movie events.

Leonardo DiCaprio

Leonardo DiCaprio is a famous Hollywood actor who has starred in many popular movies. He worked very hard for many years before winning his first Oscar award. Leonardo also cares deeply about the Earth and often speaks about protecting nature and animals.
